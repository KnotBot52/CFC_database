Cruelty-Free Products Directory

Carmine Red Lipstick

Not Vegan

This classic red lipstick achieves its vibrant color using carmine, a red pigment derived from crushed cochineal insects. It also contains lanolin derived from sheep's wool.

Green Earth Moisturizer

Vegan

This product contains zero animal-derived ingredients. The squalane used is plant-derived (from olives) rather than from shark liver oil, and the company has a strict no-animal-testing policy verified by Leaping Bunny.

Luminous Honey Glow Serum

Not Vegan

While cruelty-free (not tested on animals), this product contains honey and beeswax as active ingredients. Therefore, it is not suitable for vegans.

Mineral Sunscreen SPF 50

Vegan

This mineral sunscreen uses zinc oxide for UV protection and a blend of coconut and jojoba oils for hydration. It is 100% vegan and reef-safe.

Pure Leaf Shampoo

Vegan

Pure Leaf Shampoo is certified vegan. All its ingredients are derived directly from botanical extracts, and they use a synthetic preservative system that contains no animal byproducts.
